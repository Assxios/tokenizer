Access [remix](https://remix.ethereum.org) and select your workspace:
![remix](/documentation/imgs/remix.png)

Once you are done coding, you can compile the contract by clicking on the `Solidity Compiler` tab on the left and then clicking on the `Compile` button:
![compile](/documentation/imgs/compile.png)

Then, you can deploy the contract by clicking on the `Deploy & run transactions` tab on the left and then clicking on the `Deploy` button:  
![deploy](/documentation/imgs/deploy.png)
> **You will have to be on the `Sepolia` test network in Metamask to deploy the contract.**   
Make sure you use `Metamask` as the injected provider for your environment and that you select your contract in the `Contract` dropdown menu.

Your contract is now deployed on the `Sepolia` test network!
